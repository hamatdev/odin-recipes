Strawberries and Cream Oatmeal

Description

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups diced strawberries, divided
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar, or to taste
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 cup oatmeal
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Place ¾ cup strawberries, milk, water, brown sugae, and salt in a blender cup and blend until smooth.
  2. Place oatmeal and strawberry mixture in a saucepan, bring to boil.
  3. Reduce the heat, add remaining strawberries, and simmer until thick.
  4. About 5 minutes, remove from heat; Stir in vanilla.
