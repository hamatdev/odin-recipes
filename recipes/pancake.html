Whole Wheat Pancake Mix

Description

This is a bulk whole wheat pancake mix recipe that your family is going to absolutely love.

It is very easy to make and also make pretty good waffles

Ingredients

  • 5 cups whole wheat flour
  • 3 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 2½ tablespoons baking powder
  • 4 teaspoons baking soda
  • 4 teaspoons salt

Steps

  1. Whisk whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl.
  2. Store in an airtight container.
  3. To use:
    • Mix 1 cup of pancake mix, 1 cup of milk, 1 egg, and 2 tablespoons of oil together in a large bowl.
    • Cook pancakes on a hot griddle and serve with your choice of toppings
